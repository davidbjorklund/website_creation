View > Garden and Hand Tools Since 1760 Spear and Jackson has been selling hand, garden, contractors, agricultural, landscaping and professional tools from its base in the city of Sheffield. Over 250 years of experience and continuous innovation has built an enviable reputation and a market leading product range that offers high quality and performance tools to suit a wide range of needs. Garden Tools & Outdoor Living Our comprehensive range of garden tools and outdoor living products provides everything you need to keep your garden looking perfect. With products suitable for all abilities and budgets, we’re confident we have the tools you need. View products Hand & Contractor Tools Screwdrivers, plastering trowels and insulated tools are just some of the products in our extensive range of hand and contractors’ tools. Designed with performance, safety and durability in mind, you can be sure of a quality result for any DIY and professional tasks. View products Eclipse Professional Tools Established over 100 years ago in 1909 the Eclipse brand is recognised worldwide as the professionals’ choice for metal cutting and machinery workshop tools. Comprising market leading ranges of hand and power hacksaw blades, small saw blades and hacksaw frames, over the years the brand has expanded to include full ranges of Eclipse engineer’s hand tools, lathe tools, riveters, holesaws and toolbits and other professional workshop tools.
