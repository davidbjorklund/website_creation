High-Quality Industrial Grade - Air Jacks and Cylinder Locks Emerson focuses on developing our products to the highest of standards. Our quality Air Jacks are designed to lift with speed while also safely locking into place. Our Air Jack Models 110 and 220 work well for lifting trailers, tractors and other various equipment for tire and brake maintenance. While most cylinder locks are permanently attached once in use, Emerson’s can be removed and reused for various purposes. Our CL-30 Locks come in various sizes that fit on one single wheel cart making storage a snap. Professional Transmission Jacks for Truck, Auto and Diesel Mechanics Emerson understands that technicians have limited space to work in at times. That is why our Meyer Hydraulic Transmission Jacks have refined aerodynamics to account for new truck design changes and limited workspaces. Now mechanics no longer have to fight against air tanks, fairings and other components.
