The company has become one of the world’s leading manufacturers of rollers and harrows. Dal-Bo continues to lead the way in relation to the functions and capacities of rollers. Now, the journey towards the implementation of a new ambitious strategy commences under the management of Anja Plougmann, Chairman. The springboard will be the present platform which is founded on 70 years’ experience with the development, manufacture and sale of soil cultivation implements. Part of this journey involves a generational shift, in which the present management resigns to make room for a new management team headed by Carsten Abel as CEO/Managing Director. The new management team also includes   Torben Norlyk Pedersen as Sales Manager and Bo Toft Hansen, Production Manager, as well as the Country Managers of Dal-Bo’s sales companies in Germany, Great Britain and France. Mr. Kaj Pedersen who has played a central role in the development of Dal-Bo for 50 years, supports the new management team for the period of transition.   The changes have no specific impact on the British organisation which is headed by Steve Atkin, Sales Manager.
