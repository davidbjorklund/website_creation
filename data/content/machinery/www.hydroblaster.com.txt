Hydroblaster. We built our first Hydroblaster pressure washer in 1980 and since then have developed hundreds of different models and configurations. Hydroblasters are among the safest pressure washers in the world. They are certified to UL and CSA standards and conform to CE standards. Hydroblaster pumps can be powered by electric motors, gasoline or diesel engines, and pneumatic motors. Systems may be stationary when provisioned with skid or floor mounts or portable on casters, tires or trailer mounting. Hydroblaster water heating systems may be fueled with electricity, oil (diesel, kerosene, JP4 or JP8), LP gas or natural gas. With water flows up to 60 GPM and pressures to 5000 PSI, it is likely that we have just the system for your application. We can a deliver pressure washer that is safe, reliable and will meet your needs.
