Discover more   Slitter Rewinders and Converting Machinery ASHE Converting Equipment is an independent manufacturer of premium quality slitter rewinders and customised applications. Based in the UK, we’ve been producing and supplying slitting machines and converting machinery to the global film, paper and allied web industries for over 30 years. The latest in high speed technology We are constantly pushing the boundaries of technology so that you can enjoy safer, more productive slitter rewinders that deliver a superior finish even on sensitive materials. Choose from a wide range of standard products and bespoke equipment and benefit from the high level of technical expertise that’s goes into every machine: High tech machines exclusive to the converting industry Hydraulic-free, so ideal for cleanroom applications Feature rich, easy to use equipment for higher output Twin shaft technology for minimal downtime Health and safety compliant Standard or Bespoke Our skilled mechanical and software engineers work side by side to continually progress our slitter rewinders and converting equipment, from the basic to the fully automatic machines. As well as being innovation specialists, they also develop and manufacture electronic drives and motor control systems, including DC drives, AC inverters and servo drives. Tailored Solutions This expert knowledge means we can also design and build customised high productivity slitter rewinders to meet your precise needs. Previous creations have included turret winders with unique widths and features. We’ve also manufactured slitting machines for special applications, such as copper foil, which require particularly heavy duty machines. We also stock a full range of accessories, including slitting equipment, trim winders, differential shafts and differential core chucks. So whether you need individual parts or a full installation, you can rely on us to supply you with quality slitter rewinders and converting equipment. Exceptional after sales care The full range of slitter rewinders are made in-house by our engineering and manufacturing team. They, alongside our electronics division, are on hand to give you a full support service, wherever you are in the world. After installation you will receive full training in the usage of any purchased slitter rewinders and enjoy ongoing support from our technicians. Any problems are overcome quickly with a simple telephone call or email which will get you back on track, fast. That way you will benefit from reliable, 24 hour production.
