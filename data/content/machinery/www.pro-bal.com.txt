Pro-Bal Balancers             Pro-Bal Balancers is the market leader in the manufacture and supply of computerized electronic dynamic hard bearing balancers for automotive and industrial applications. We offer a very broad range of over 50 standard models including Horizontal and Vertical machines. These units are designed to balance parts including armatures and rotors, printing and textile rolls, gears, pulleys, spindles, impellers, crankshafts, driveshafts, flywheels and more.
