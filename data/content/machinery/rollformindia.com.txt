About RollForm India Rollform is India's leading supplier of turn key solutions for Coil Processing, Tube Manufacturing and Tube Processing Serving the Steel Tube & Pipe industry since 1992, Rollform is synonymous for top of the line quality and high technology equipment. With many installations worldwide, Rollform has a host of highly satisfied clients who are getting maximum yields and high volume production from their equipment. Rollform has a very active collaboration with a German company M/s Manfred Olk Rohrwerkstechnik GmbH & Co. KG, Germany. All our machines are designed in Germany and are manufactured as such in India in conformance to European standards.
