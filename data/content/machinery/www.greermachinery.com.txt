We offer new, refurbished and used lathes, and assist in the sales and services of Mori Seiki and Hwacheon lathes. Our family has been in the machinery industry since 1929. Tom Jr.’s grandfather opened the first location in Los Angeles. Then, in 1984, Tom Greer Jr. opened Greer Machinery Co. in Huntington Beach, California. Now ran by the third generation, Greer Machinery is still committed to continue conducting business with the same integrity and honest representation of machinery as their forefathers. Since then, Greer Machinery Co. has sold over 2,000 lathes to satisfied customers across the country and has become the number #1 importer for Hwacheon.
