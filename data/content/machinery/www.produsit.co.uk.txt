Jigs, Fixtures, Toolmaking Manufacturers and Machining Services <body> Produsit Ltd is a long established precision engineering and toolmaking Company originally founded in 1947 in Birmingham. We specialise in the design and manufacture of jigs, fixtures, prototypes, machining from plastics or metals, and special purpose machines. Our customer base covers the automotive, aerospace, railways and electrical industries. We also manufacture drying racks for the screen printing industry, schools, etc. The Company has at it's disposal a full range of machine tools, both manual and CNC, together with fabrication and assembly facilities, and can offer a total manufacturing service which may include, pneumatics, hydraulics, and electronics control </body>
