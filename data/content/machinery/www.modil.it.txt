MODELLERIA MODIL: DESIGN AND CONSTRUCTION OF MODELS FOR MOULDS Founded in 1975, the Modil Pattern-making Company’s experience in making moulds for the footwear sector stretches over thirty years. And although thirty years ago mould manufacturing required hands-on craftsmanship throughout, nowadays technology and progress have brought radical changes with them, in this sector as in many others, and Modil has not been slow in taking advantage of them. With a combination of cutting edge technology and experience it has kept up with the times designing cad-cam stations with computerised NC systems that deliver high precision and the sophisticated finishing touches of the master craftsman that leaves no detail to chance. The company’s mission statement is to supply its customers with all the valid assistance and advice they need to create, design and construct a model that will also satisfy in terms of quality, price and delivery times with all the professional skill, reliability and experience that its years in the sector guarantee.
