Heinz Focke. His spirit of invention and original business strategy still characterizes the company and is the basis for its continued success on the packaging machine market. Dr. h. c. Heinz Focke’s two-pronged corporate philosophy was as simple as convincing: He wanted to provide packaging machines that conform to the highest quality standards and stand out for their efficient production, reliability and longevity. He insisted to design these machines according to the highest standards of engineering and produce them with the help of the very best manufacturing methods. FOCKE & CO has lived up to these principles throughout the decades. The company has repeatedly set new benchmarks in terms of output and product quality.
