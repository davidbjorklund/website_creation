We maintain a multi-million dollar inventory of new and refurbished OEM parts in order to provide a high quality product and short delivery to our customers. In addition, GL&V has a highly skilled, OEM trained, team of process and mechanical engineers to provide advice and recommendations to optimize our customer’s operations and equipment reliability. Repair & Rebuild GL&V Pulp and Paper offers a complete program of services to repair and rebuild OEM equipment. Our objective is to rebuild your equipment to the original OEM specifications, in order to assure you get the extended life and maximum operating efficiency out of your machinery. To minimize your downtime, we have developed an extensive exchange program for many of our products. Our R&R team is extremely informed and dedicated in finding solutions for our customers.
