Festival Strings receive another Strad Violins of the ensemble founders reunited The fact that the last musician to play the „Sellière“ Stradivarus was none other than the co-founder of Lucerne Festival Strings, Wolfgang Schneiderhan, has many implications for the character of the ensemble. The addition of the aforementioned instrument completes the circle, as the other Stradivarius that has been loaned to the ensemble used to belong to co-founder Rudolf Baumgartner. These instruments are more than mere prestige, as they tell a story deeply rooted in the history of the ensemble. German Tour June 2019 Berlin | Braunschweig | Hamburg | Hannover German Tour 2019   We’ll be on tour in Germany together with Kit Armstrong from the 11th until the 16th of June, with stops at the Berliner Konzerthaus at the Gendarmenmarkt in the prominently attended concert series “Kammerorchester International”, the Elbphilharmonie in Hamburg in which we will be playing for the fourth time since 2017, Hannover as well as Braunschweig. SEE MORE Concert Season 2018/19 Brochure     For the 2018/19 season, the Lucerne Festival Strings are presenting its own concert series at Lucerne KKL and being invited to Lucerne Festival. Being on tour the orchestra is heading for Hamburg's Elbphilharmonie twice, as well as to Berlin, to Vienna and to Bologna. The list of soloists includes Ray Chen, Arabella Steinbacher, Jan Lisiecki, Kit Armstrong and Alban Gerhardt.
