From the age of four a child's fancy with a Hawaiian guitar evolves into creating patented guitars and unique playing style that has been mesmerizing audiences and artists world over for decades. Global recognition of the patented guitars Chaturangui , Gandharvi and Anandi , which not only excel in sound variety and quality, have also become popular instruments. With the lineage of Gurus like Pandit Brij Bhushan Kabra and Pandit Ajoy Chakrabarty the inspiration has gone beyond performance.
