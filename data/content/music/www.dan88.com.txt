Dan has started work on two more artistic recordings for CD release. Double Standard is a reflection of the much-loved Bill Evans album Conversations With Myself . The work sees Dan recording two live overdubbed pianos with selections from the American Songbook, as well as original compositions. Eclecto-maniac is a high-energy trip into thematic music, where the intention of the artist is to evoke primitive emotions in the listener.
