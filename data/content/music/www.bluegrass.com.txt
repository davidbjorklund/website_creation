Learn More 01 02 03 Scroll for more Colorado Music Festivals & Concerts Colorado Music Festivals & Concerts Colorado Music Festivals & Concerts For over 25 years, Planet Bluegrass has been redefining the music festival, creating exceptional musical experiences that protect our planet through environmental leadership and a strong community. Each of our 3 festivals ( Telluride Bluegrass Festival , RockyGrass , and Folks Festival ) continues to make a unique musical mark: each taking place in breathtaking natural Colorado environments; each featuring the world’s finest musicians and songwriters; and each embracing a “Leave No Trace” ethic that has defined Planet Bluegrass as a leader in Sustainable Festivation *.
