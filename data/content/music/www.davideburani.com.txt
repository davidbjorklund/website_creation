He was subsequently awarded a diploma in harp from the Conservatory “Arrigo Boito” of Parma, where he studied under the guidance of Francesca Frigotto and Emanuela Degli Esposti. He completed postgraduate studies in harp with Ieuan Jones at the Royal College of London, with Fabrice Pierre and Judith Liber. He has participated in numerous national and international competitions, finalist at the International Competition "Tournoi International de Musique" - XI edition, with  honorable mention.
