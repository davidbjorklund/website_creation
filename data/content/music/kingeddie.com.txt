King Eddie and Pili Pili – reggae King Eddie & Pili Pili For many years King Eddie has led the popular Jacksonville Reggae band “Pili Pili” which was named after the Swahili word for “hot sauce.” Pili Pili is the featured performer at the Ritz Carlton, Amelia Island, Florida weekly pool parties and on the local concert circuit has performed on the same bill with the Crusaders, Miles Davis, Spyra Gyra, Branford Marsalis and Third World, to name a few. With his first album King Eddie moves from Jacksonville to the World with a “killer track” that will fill the dance floor. King Eddie and Pili Pili have long been popular with the Northeast Florida music fans. Amongst the Reggae bands of the area they shine like a bright star with personality, great sounds, and undeniable audience appeal.
