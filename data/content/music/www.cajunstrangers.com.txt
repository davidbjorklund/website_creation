Cajun Strangers - Cajun Music from Madison, WI The Cajun Strangers are a Cajun music band from Madison, Wisconsin. The Cajun Strangers latest album, Louisiana Boogie, features Brian O'Donnell on fiddle, Tom Nickel on accordion, Boo Mullarky on guitars, Joel Johnson on bass, and Colin Bazsali on drums.
