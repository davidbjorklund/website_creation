The Dessoff Choirs celebrates its 95th year with performances of choral masterworks by both 19th- and 20th-century luminaries and today’s most innovative composers. Our season features the Fauré Requiem in its original 1893 version, works by John Rutter and Gregg Smith , the New York premiere of Craig Hella Johnson’s Considering Matthew Shepard, and a reprise performance of The Ballad of the Brown King to celebrate the 2020 release of our Margaret Bonds CD! Also, save the date of October 17, 2019 for another exciting evening with Dessoff singers Off the Risers !   Details to follow in September. We are very grateful for your patronage and support during our 2018–2019 season and look forward to singing for you again in October 2019 as we launch Dessoff@95 ! Tickets available here . Your support is instrumental! Your gift to The Dessoff Choirs today will help make extraordinary music available to music lovers like you throughout the year. Please donate now!
