Blanchardstown Brass Band, established 1826, one of Ireland's oldest brass bands Search Home History History Historical Timeline The Blanchardstown Sound Past Members Upcoming Performances Photo Gallery 1920s – 1940s 1950s – 1960s 1970s – 1990s 2000s – Present Facebook Photos from 2012 Video Gallery Contact Us   Home Follow @BlanchBrassBand Welcome to the home of the Blanchardstown Brass Band, Dublin, one of the oldest bands in Ireland. The band have been performing at events and venues around Dublin and beyond for 193 years now. After being established in 1826 as the St. Brigid’s Brass & Reed Band, the name was changed in 1971 to reflect the all-brass nature of the band. We are always on the look out for new players, especially experienced players, who may be interested in joining, just visit the ‘Contact Us’ page for further details or use the contact form below. The band is committed to fostering an interest in brass music in the youth of the community and we regularly run juvenille beginner classes, again just visit the ‘Contact Us’ page for further details. Supported by Fingal County Council through the Community Enhancement Programme 2018. Presented with funding from the Music Network Music Capital Scheme, funded by the Department of Arts, Heritage and the Gaeltacht. Scoil Bride Brass Band Project Blanchardstown has been supported by IPB Insurance through the IPB Youth & Community Fund which forms part of its Corporate Social Engagement Framework.
