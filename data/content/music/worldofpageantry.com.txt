BEST!!! /.item /.carousel-inner /.carousel /#main-slider div class="gap"></div The World of Pageantry   The World of Pageantry is the premier internet pageantry arts resource, covering the west coast's marching band, drum corps, drumline, color guard and drill team activities.  The Pageantry Forums, known as the Pageantry Discussion Group, is the largest such forum on the web, boasting over 8,000 members currently. For many years the World of Pageantry was the leading west coast print media dedicated the the marching pageantry arts, but with rise of the internet it has changed with the times and become a leading internet presence and advocate for the Pageantry Arts.
