When you register on Musicalics.com, you will be able to subscribe to the monthly newsletter . You will also be able to edit pages on the site or add new content , depending on your wishes. Are you a composer? When you are (representing) a composer, and you are already listed on this site, then let me know. I will give you an account with permissions to control the content on your composer and composition pages. You can open an account here .
