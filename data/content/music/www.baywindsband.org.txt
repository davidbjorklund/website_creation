Bay Winds Band Home Directors Photos Members New Members History Upcoming Events Contact Us Welcome to the website of The Bay Winds Band of Annapolis, Maryland. Annapolis is the state capital of Maryland, the county seat of Anne Arundel County, and home of the United States Naval Academy and Saint John's College. Bay Winds is a non-profit volunteer organization which performs concerts in the Annapolis vicinity. The size of the band varies between 60 and 80 members, and rehearses and performs from September through early July. Bay Winds plays outdoor concerts in the warm weather months and indoor concerts year-round. For planned concert dates and locations, click on the Upcoming Events tab. Bay Winds Band thanks St. John's Church in Linthicum for providing storage space for our music library.
