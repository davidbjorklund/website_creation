Our Music We play mostly within the City of Adelaide council area, about once a month, usually on Sunday afternoons; in addition to other public & private events around Adelaide. Rehearsals The band rehearses on Thursday evenings from 7:30 pm to 9:30 pm at St Stephen's Lutheran Church, 152 Wakefield St, Adelaide, SA 5000. New members and visitors are always welcome to attend. A few key people The band is governed by a committee consisting of members of the City of Adelaide Band.
