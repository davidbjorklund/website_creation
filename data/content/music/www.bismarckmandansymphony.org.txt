Today the BMSO presents eight annual themed subscription concerts plus the popular Fourth of July Spectacular. Each concert is unique, filled with engaging musical performances featuring skilled guest artists from around the country. Events Meet the Conductor Meet the Musicians Education The Bismarck-Mandan Symphony Orchestra believes in promoting self-expression and community awareness through music educational programs. We strive to inspire students and participants to come away from these programs believing that music can uplift themselves as well as their community. Youth Orchestra Education Concerts Young Artist Competition The Historic Belle Mehus Welcome to the Bismarck-Mandan Orchestral Association's elegant performance home, the Belle Mehus City Auditorium, in downtown Bismarck. We strive to inspire joy and build our community through music and the performing arts.
