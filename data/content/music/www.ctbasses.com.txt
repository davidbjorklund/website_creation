He has made instruments for such greats as Stanley Clarke, Anthony Jackson and Les Claypool, as well as created the first electric 6-string bass, piccolo bass, set-neck heel block design and was the first to experiment with different scale lengths. All of his instruments are custom order, hand crafted and completely unique; there is no assembly line. Styles range from four string piccolo basses, to guitars, all the way to six string basses and even more exotic instruments.
