Flutes On The Brink Flute Choir FLUTES ON THE BRINK FLUTE CHOIR, MONTGOMERY COUNTY, MARYLAND Flutes on the Brink is a flute choir centered in Montgomery County, MD, consisting of a mixture of about 20 professional and amateur musicians. For most of the year, we rehearse every Monday, at 7:30, at Mill Creek Parish United Methodist Church, 7101 Horizon Terrace, Derwood, MD.
