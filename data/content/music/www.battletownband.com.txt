History The original band dates back well into the 19th Century. It was dissolved in 1939, at the onset of the Second World War. Research is underway into the band’s early years. The band was reformed in 1975, based around students from a brass evening class run by the then director Angela Holland. Progress was rapid, with the band growing in number and strength...
