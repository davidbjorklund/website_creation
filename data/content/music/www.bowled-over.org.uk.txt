We are Karen (baritone), Elaine (bass), Lindsey (lead) and Jo (tenor).   We all sing with local Ladies Barbershop Choruses.  As a quartet: We are Bowled Over!   In October 2013 we were NATIONAL FINALISTS coming SIXTH and in 2010 we won the BRONZE MEDAL at the Ladies Association of Barbershop Singers Convention! WHY BOWLED OVER? Our quartet started life in the Three Spires Bowling Club where our chorus rehearsed. Proudly powered by Weebly
