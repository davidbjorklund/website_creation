Washington Post, September 27, 2010 Upcoming Performances of the Season: Muses Sing! A concert of the art songs by the women composers Bel Cantanti Opera opens its sixteenth 2019-2020 season with a concert of the art songs by four women composers: Alma Mahler, Clara Schumann (Wieck), Pauline Viardot-Garcia and Fanny Mendelssohn. Their music is not known as widely as the music of the men in their lives: Gustav Mahler, Robert Schumann, Felix Mendelssohn and Frederic Chopin. These art songs were written for voice with the piano accompaniment. But in this concert the songs will be orchestrated and singers will perform them, accompanined by cello, piano, harp, oboe and organ. The concert includes the poetry reading, projection of the fine art of the beginning of 20th century. Come to enjoy this unique concert and hear the beautiful music, which has'nt been heard enough and so soon forgotten.
