May 9th, 2019 by Ben Really excited to be unleashing new music this summer! It’s been 22 years since my last actual solo project. Visit my store to check them out. ACORNS is my version of old time string-band music. Mostly songs I wrote, mostly new, some older ones reinvented for fiddle, banjo, mando. It was recorded live in my studio with a crew of my favorite people over several joy filled days. TOOLSHED is all the other stuff I love to do — Bluesy, funk, dixie, folk — more like a laboratory experiment, where I dawned my safely glasses and lab coat and flexed my studio editing/mixing chops. Sign up info Here Posted in News | No Comments » January 6th, 2019 by Ben Posted in News | No Comments » Pass the Gravy October 27th, 2018 by Ben Most of the leaves have dropped after a glorious fall in Teton Valley. There’s a dusting of snow above 8000’ and the smell of winter in the air. The locals are busily buttoning down the hatches, raking leaves, hunting (with hopes of filling freezers) and putting away lawn furniture. Here on Birch Road, we are planting next year’s garlic and putting the garden to bed. I am also deep into putting a small addition on to my studio (the Henhouse), so as to provide an additional isolation room and more storage space. This will allow for for more flexibility when recording larger groups and will be put to use in fairly short order with some exciting projects coming up: Natalie Padilla , the One Ton Pig , and two recordings of my own (one w/ Eli West). Pass the gravy. Posted in News | No Comments » MAY DAY, MAY DAY May 1st, 2018 by Ben Spring has brought a variety of work and stuff to do that highlights all the extremes that come with living in the Tetons. Snow in the mountains and daffodils in the yard. Skiing corn one day, fishing or biking the next, and skiing powder the next (today – May 1st). Camping in the desert one weekend, and playing at the AlaskaFolk Festival in Juneau the next. I have had a number of mixing and recording projects in the Henhouse, plus one film soundtrack, AND I’m getting closer on two recording projects of my own.  Looking forward to upcoming summer gigs with John Lowell (Growling Old Men), Eli West, Jackson 6 (dixieland) and others. January 1st, 2018 by Ben Greetings and Happy New Year. Here’s hoping for Peace and Health (both yours and the world’s) and less political turmoil. 2017 was a good year for me and I’m optimistic that 2018 will be even better. One of the highlights of this past year was the completion of Owen Thomas Winship’s debut CD. Owen is my oldest son. He is a circus performer (currently in London performing in the musical, Barnum ) and a part-time singer songwriter. The EP features 7 original songs with backing by Eli West, Eric Thorin, Margo Valiante, myself and others. More info here .
