The ANTHOLOGY  CD comprises original Dulahan Civil War songs released on prior albums plus a newly recorded song “BOY SOLDIERS”. This compilation/collection will be officially released the weekend of the Dayton Celtic Festival (last weekend in July 2019)  so mark your calendars for  a great weekend of fun at DCF plus celebrate with Dulahan the release of this new special collection CD. Dulahan will be performing all three  days at DCF. Admission is Free so don’t miss the fun!   Celtic Fest Ohio 2019 Come join the fun at Celtic Fest Ohio  the weekend of  June 14, 15 & 16 on the spacious grounds of  Renaissance Park in Waynesville Ohio. This will be the 4th year Dulahan has performed at the fest and every year it gets bigger and better. Dulahan will play 3 shows (2 saturday and 1 sunday)  and all three will be at the Aleing Knight Pub Stage. Satudays  shows are at 1:30 PM and 6:30PM  and sundays show is at 3:15 PM. Come enjoy all the sights, sounds, artisans, cultural exhibits, food and drink as the Renaissance Festival grounds are turned into a family friendly  Celtic  experience. Multiple stages for entertainment throughout the fest keep the music and dance flowing non stop so make plans to join Dulahan for a fantastic weekend at Celtic Fest Ohio. Highland Stag Pubs 2 Year Anniversary Party Come celebrate the Highland Stag Pubs 2nd Anniversary  with Dulahan on Saturday May 18th . The Stag is a fun intimate atmosphere to take in  a Dulahan show  and the pub has a great selection of food and drink and the show starts at 7 PM sharp  so get there early and grab some grub. You can also reserve a table if you call ahead so we hope you’ll make plans to  join us for a roof raising time on Saturday May 18th  in Springboro, Ohio.
