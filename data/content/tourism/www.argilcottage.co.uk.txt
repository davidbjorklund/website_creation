Whether it's just the two of you or with family or friends, you'll love this renovated 100-year-old detached cottage in a little village near Castle Howard. Right between the traditional market towns of Kirkbymoorside, Pickering and Helmsley, it's just 40 minutes to York, Thirsk and the coast at Whitby, this 4-Star Gold Award country cottage is perfectly placed for you to explore the North York Moors.
