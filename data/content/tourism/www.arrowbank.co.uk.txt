On the edge of the picture postcard, black and white village of Eardisland, this family owned holiday park is at one with natural beauty of its’ surroundings. Whether yours is a short visit with touring caravan or motorhome or a longer stay in one of our caravan holiday homes or self-catering lodges you will feel immediately relaxed.   The sense of peace and beauty is guaranteed and Arrow Bank also offers the choice to stroll in to the village and visit the tea rooms or local inns or to enjoy riverside walks to the nearby village of Pembridge. Perhaps try your hand at fishing on the River Arrow meandering through the park or you may just prefer to soak in the tranquillity of this haven in the country. What to do…? 5 Star Rated Relax and unwind at Arrow Bank A peaceful 5 star caravan holiday park with a get away from it all atmosphere. A riverside holiday park nestled in glorious Herefordshire countryside with everything you need close to hand - local shop, pub and tea rooms. The River Arrow is a little gem of a river that gently winds its way through the Herefordshire countryside; running along the boundary of Arrow Bank Holiday Park, where you can fish for free, winding its way through the pretty black and white village of Eardisland. The village of Eardisland is just a short level walk from the park. The local community of is proud of this beautiful North Herefordshire village, which attracts over 20,000 visitors a year. It has been described as one of England's quintessential, timeless villages that inspires visitors.
