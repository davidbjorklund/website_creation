Located on the quieter end of historic Duval Street, walking distance to all Key West has to offer, our 19 room boutique hotel offers several room types. Stay just one block away from the Southernmost Point in the continental USA and only steps away from the beach. Whether you are looking for a romantic getaway or this is just one stop on your Florida tour we are sure to have the right accommodations for you. Locally owned and operated for over 20 years, this circa 1885 property has been lovingly restored to offer modern amenities while maintaining the colonial ambiance that is Key West. Enjoy breakfast on the porch each morning or sip a glass of wine by the pool deck in the evening, all the while knowing you are just minutes away from the hustle and bustle of Duval Street's nightlife and the famous Sunset Celebration at Mallory Square. The Key West Butterfly & Nature Conservatory is just across the street, and attractions such as the Ernest Hemingway House and the Lighthouse Museum are only a leisurely stroll away. So what are you waiting for?
