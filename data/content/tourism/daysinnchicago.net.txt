Located in Chicago's vibrant Lincoln Park and Lakeview neighborhood, Hotel Versey connects travelers with the local culture, music, art and food of the Windy City. A bold and eclectic new design and local art merge with our storied jazz and rock history to create a reimagined hotel. Hotel Versey's opening and interior design were celebrated on Architectural Digest's prestigious list of "The 12 Best Designed Hotels Opening This Year." Walk to diverse communities like Boystown and Lakeview and iconic attractions like the Lincoln Park Zoo, Lake Michigan and Wrigley Field.  Don't just visit Chicago, live it with Hotel Versey .
