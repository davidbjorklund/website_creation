Glen Isla House is nestled amongst 2 acres of heritage gardens and oak trees of the historic Glen Isla homestead (c1870), and has direct beach access.  All accommodations are purpose-designed as guest accommodations, feature elegant and unique interiors, a flat-screen TV and beautiful authentic heritage garden views. For guests seeking more spacious accommodadions, an historic suite with a spa bath and fireplace is available, and there is a 2-bedroom self-cater cottage option.  (We advise that our accommodations are unsuitable for infants and young children) Glen Isla House is a 15-minute drive from Phillip Island Nature Parks, where you can see wildlife including the world famous little penguins at the Penguin Parade (nightly at dusk), koalas up close and personal at the Koala Conservation Centre, and Australian fur seals off the Nobbies (by boat tour).  Phillip Island offers numerous nature walks, world-class bird-watching and a selection of swimming & surf beaches. Phillip Island Grand Prix Circuit is 15-minutes' drive, and Melbourne is a 2-hour drive from Glen Isla. Like the beach?   A pristine sandy beach beckons just 100 metres directly out the back gate...
