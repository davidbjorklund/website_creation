The ocean has always been a place for reflection and renewal.  We hope you make many wonderful memories at the beach and are pleased to offer professionally managed vacation properties with all the comforts of home.  We are committed to providing each of our guests with an exceptional experience during their stay with us.   Our vacation home selection includes spacious one and two bedroom condos, all with full kitchens, high speed wifi and internet, flatscreen TV's, cookware, washer/dryer, luxury linens and towels.  Many of our homes also accept well behaved dogs. The Westport and South Beach is the perfect place to get away.  We have 18 miles of pristine sandy coastline, popular surf breaks, a gorgeous working marina (with an active commercial and charter fishing fleet), Washington's tallest Lighthouse, and great small town dining and shopping.  You're sure to find lots of activities to satisfy your need for beach therapy.  Whether your idea of a great vacation includes curling up with a good book, catching some waves, or reeling in the big one, we have something for everyone. Beautiful Ocean Views, Spectacular Settings, Family Fun Whether just for a night, a weekend, or hopefully longer, thank you for staying with Vacation by the Sea and we look forward to helping you plan a fanastic trip.
