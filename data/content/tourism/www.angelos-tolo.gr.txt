Take a break, plan your holiday in Greece on the beach. For holidays in Tolo, Greece, Angelos Apartments or Kyani Akti Hotel are a fine choice offering reasonably priced double rooms, studios and apartments a few steps from the  "Sandy Beach" of Tolo. Take advantage of our friendly, family run hotel and studio flats found in the popular resort of Tolo (Tolon) in the Argolida county of the Peloponnese in southern Greece. Choose either our double hotel rooms or on a self catering basis in our studios and apartments for more independence, enjoy the close proximity to the sea and have the opportunity to visit the wonderful Greek historical sites of the area such as Mycenae & the Ancient Theatre of Epidavros. Tolo's Sandy Bech is ideal for swimming, snorkelling, watersports and sunbathing for all ages - the sea is shallow and perfect for children. Visit our rental sunbeds and umbrellas and get to know "Angelos", one of your hosts. Tolo is easily accessible from the airports of Athens and Kalamata which makes it a popular resort for both Greeks and foreign tourists all year round and with the first capital of Greece, Nafplion just 10kms away visitors are never short of something to do and see no matter the season.
