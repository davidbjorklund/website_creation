::Island View Bed & Breakfast:: Wiarton, Ontario   Island View B & B rests on twelve acres of the Niagara Escarpment   overlooking Colpoys Bay. The famous Bruce Trail is a 5 minute walk from our front door. Walk to the scenic Bruce's Caves in all seasons. We are 200 metres from groomed snowmobile trails, cross country ski trails and snowshoeing. Enjoy beautiful sunrises over the islands and sunsets over the bay. Relax beside the fireplace in our cedar sunroom.   Original Artwork by Steven Young on display.
