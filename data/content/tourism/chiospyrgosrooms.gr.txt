Chios is the ideal place for your holidays. It is the island of mastic and medieval villages and above all the island with a stunning variety of landscapes. The Avgonyma village belongs to the municipality of Omiroupoli and it is 16 km far from the town center of Chios. It is a medieval village of the 11th century, built by the technicians of Nea Moni who used it for their installation after the completion of the monastery. From one side it has a view to the big and evergreen forest of Provatas mountain and from the other side to the crystal blue waters of the Aegean Sea reaching up to Evia. It is only 10 minutes from the beautiful and pristine beaches of the west coast of Chios. In the evening you can enjoy the magnificent sunset.
