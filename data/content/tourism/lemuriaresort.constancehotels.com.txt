Opened in December 1999, this luxury hotel was fully renovated and then reopened in November 2016. Built with a modern approach to design and quality, Constance Lemuria live up to the expectations of guests who enjoy pure luxury and a preserved nature.   .hf-text-content-hotels GREEN GLOBE Certified Exclusivity Enjoy elegant spacious suites and villas or unwind in swimming pools seemingly crafted by nature.
