End Profile End People End Rates Section Amenities Section Title Page Activities End Title Page People Start Profile About Provincetown Provincetown has a great deal to see and do and has even been rated by Fodor's as one of the 10 Best Small Towns in America . Dyer's Beach House offers you an inviting atmosphere as a Provincetown waterfront guest house. Let us help you enjoy your time with us. Bicycle through the majestic beach forests along the national seashore, Bicycles can be rented in town, golf and tennis are close bye as well. Kayak the Provincetown Harbor, boats can be rented along the beach. Sail the sunset on the bay, charters are available within walking distance. Whale watching is legendary off the coastal tidal waters of New England with charters sailing around the feeding times of the local sea life off the harbor. See some of the most beautiful sunsets on the east coast from Race Point on a dune tour . All this and so much more is here for you. Let Dyer's Beach House, make your vacation a memorable one in one of our waterfront guest rooms. In 2012, we began a winter project in support of local artists allowing 1 Provincetown artist to use our space for the winter to paint in exchange for the presenting their artwork in our guest rooms at in Provincetown boutique motel for the summer. You can expect to see a fresh look in the guest rooms every year. This year, we present to you Peg Reynolds . Please visit her website and then see her work in person this summer.
