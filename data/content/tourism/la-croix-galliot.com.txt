About Taillebois family Jean-Michel was born and raised on the farm of la Croix-Galliot , travelled a lot and met Kathi from Austria. We came to settle here in 2006 in the house of our parents, which is established as a guest house Brittany since 1992 . We have two girls of 6 and 8years old. We raise some chicken for the eggs, a cat and 3 black sheep . We take care of our environment.
