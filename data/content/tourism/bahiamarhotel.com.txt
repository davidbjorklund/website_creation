View Terms Book Now At A Glance Experience a spectacular waterfront destination, panoramic views, and the beach at Bahia Mar Fort Lauderdale Beach - a DoubleTree by Hilton. A beautiful getaway between the Intracoastal Waterway and the Atlantic Ocean, our hotel is ideally located in the ‘The Venice of America’. Begin your stay with a signature DoubleTree Chocolate Chip Cookie at Bahia Mar. We offer two accommodation buildings: The Marina Wing with standard and Marina view guest rooms and the Bahia Tower, rising 16 stories, providing Partial Ocean and Ocean view rooms. Enjoy multiple dining options including Breezes, Waves Rooftop Bar & Grill, and the High Tide Lounge. For quick snacks, The Market has a variety of beverages, snacks, sandwiches, homemade gelato, and more. Relax during your stay with our array of amenities. Enjoy fun-filled activities for the family, including water sports, sightseeing cruises, snorkeling, deep sea fishing, scuba excursions, the Jungle Queen, and more. Lounge by the pool with refreshments or enjoy live music on the patio. Our award-winning property features 25,000 sq. ft. of indoor and outdoor meeting and event space and was recognized by ConventionSouth Magazine as one of the 'Best 2019 New and Renovated Meeting Sites'. We have hosted a variety of gatherings — from stylish weddings and corporate meetings to events such as the Fort Lauderdale International Boat Show and Tortuga Music Festival. Highlights Newly reimagined and nautically designed iconic centerpiece of Fort Lauderdale Beach 25,000 sq. ft. of renovated indoor & outdoor meeting and event space On-site indoor and outdoor dining with live entertainment on weekends Rooftop pool and fully equipped fitness center World-class Marina and Yachting Center What to do around here Bahia Mar Fort Lauderdale Beach is just a few steps from the beach and in walking distance to Fort Lauderdale's well-known attractions. Guests can stroll on the beach, book a snorkeling or diving excursion, explore Bonnet House Museum & Gardens or Hugh Taylor Birch State Park. The hotel is within minutes of Las Olas Boulevard and The Galleria at Fort Lauderdale. In addition, the Fort Lauderdale/Hollywood International Airport (FLL), Broward County Convention Center, and Port Everglades Cruise Terminal are just a short drive away.
