Istanbul We are a boutique hotel at the centre of the historical peninsula of Sultanahmet. The Hotel is located within walking distance to all historical sights such as St. Sophia, the Blue Mosque, the Grand Bazaar, the Spice Market, the Hippodrome, the Obelisks, the Cistern, Topkapi Palace and many others attractions. Due to its convenient location, it offers a great advantage for guests and those who wish to explore the old city of Sultanahmet. All these major attractions are only ten minutes by foot. The Acra Hotel raises on the remains of the Byzantine Great Palace of Constantinople also known as the Sacred Palace. You can see the 2000 years old remains of the palace at our ground restaurant. The palace was served as the main residence of the Eastern Roman or Byzantine emperors from 330 to 1081 and was the centre of imperial administration for over 800 years. The hotel offers 25 guest rooms some with views to the Ancient city, others have sea views, all rooms have simple and elegant decor with a vibrant feel.
