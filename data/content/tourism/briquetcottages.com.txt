no cars are allowed on Sark end home start about About Briquet Cottages 4 star self catering accommodation These rural cottages are only a stone's throw from the west coast Perelle Bay beach. They have been extensively refurbished and upgraded to a 4 star standard. Adjacent to the cottages are a mini-market, and a garage. The cottages are situated in substantial landscaped grounds, with barbeque facilities. The island of Guernsey has many attractions including historic monuments, relaxing coastal and inland walks and 3 golf courses. Each cottage is fully equipped to a modern standard, the kitchen has a dishwasher and a washing machine, the living room has a flat screen TV & satellite connection should you wish to bring your own satellite box with you. There is one double bedroom and one twin bedroom, with all linen, towels, and hot water provided. Non-smoking throughout. end about start service Free Wi-Fi We provide free Wi-Fi to our guests so that they can keep in touch with fiends and family in the outside world. Car Parking Each of our cottages have two parking spaces reserved, with additional parking offered subject to availability. Bus route There is a nearby bus stop on the adjacent coast road, providing our guests with access to bus routes that cover the whole island. Cycle Hire We are happy to coordinate bicycle hire with a local provider for any of our guests who would like to explore Guernsey's scenic cycle routes at a leisurely pace on two wheels. Central Heating The cottages are centrally heated between October and May at no additional cost to guests. We like to keep you cosy when the Guernsey sunshine alone is not quite enough.
