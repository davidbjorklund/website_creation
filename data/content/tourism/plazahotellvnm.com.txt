With our beautiful Ballroom, meeting rooms, dining room and saloon, the Plaza is  your home for meetings, parties, conventions and other important events in northern New Mexico. The town was laid out in the traditional Spanish Colonial style, with a central plaza surrounded by buildings which could serve as fortifications in case of attack.
