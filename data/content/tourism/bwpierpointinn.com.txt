The Best Western Pier Point Inn is your ideal "home away from home" while exploring  Florence and all that the central Oregon coast has to offer. You can take a leisurely stroll along the Siuslaw River and visit the boutique shops of historic Old Town Florence.  We are close to the beach, kayaking, bike riding, golfing and sightseeing boat rides. We are also just a short drive to the very popular Sea Lion Caves and Oregon Dunes National Recreation Area. Enjoy our local culture with seasonal activities, such as the Rhododendron Festival and local sand boarding championships. After a fun day in and around Florence you can unwind and enjoy casual dining in our Bay View Bistro. We look forward to serving you!
