The countries in the southern part of the African continent are blessed with an amazing variety of breathtaking travel destinations, thrilling activities and culinary experiences. The ever-changing landscape ranges from vast deserts and endless savannas to dense African bush, indigenous forests and sky-high mountains. The rainbow of skin colour, cultures and traditions as well as religions is reflected in the welcoming nature of the people and the incredible variety of culinary delights. Here you come eye to eye with the “Big 5” (elephant, buffalo, lion, leopard and rhino) during an African safari, experience the spells of an magical “samgoma” (traditional healer) and get lost in the multitude of activities and adventures.
