The farm is set in the lovely countryside of central Argyll in the West of Scotland and occupies an elevated position, looking towards Dunadd Hill, ancient capital of the Kingdom of Dalriada and beyond that the Moine Mhor or "Great Moss" of Crinan, where the River Add winds its way to the sea We have two self-catering holiday cottages, The Byre Cottage and The Dairy Cottage, which form part of the farm square but look out onto the countryside. Each can accommodate up to four people in one twin and one double bedroom. Our rates are per cottage per week, including all electricity, towels and linen. Check in and check out are on Saturdays. See our tariff for details. At quieter times of year, short holiday breaks may be available by arrangement. Stewart & Yvonne Campbell, together with their family, run the self catering holiday cottages personally and look forward to ensuring that guests enjoy a relaxing holiday in Bonnie Argyll.
