Clovis is beautifully landscaped and provides all the comforts of your "home away from home."   Our gracefully decorated rooms offer microwave, refrigerator, writing desk, dresser, and free wireless Internet. Our outdoor swimming pool provides family fun, and a peaceful retreat during the hot summer months.   Clovis is truly inspiring with it's proximity to Yosemite, Kings Canyon and Sequoia national parks, all within a 90 mile radius. Known as California's New Frontier you will find an abundance of recreational activities to do in and around the Fresno area. Some of the outdoor activities you and your family may enjoy are; skiing, white-water rafting and rock climbing to camping, horseback riding, hiking, sailing, boating, canoing trips and kayaking.
