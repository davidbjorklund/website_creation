Situated in a quiet, safe residential area of Plettenberg Bay, Anlin Beach House offers "Four Star" affordable luxury just a mere 100 metres walk to the beautiful and unspoilt Robberg Beach ( Click to view Google Earth ). The beach is 3.5 KM long and perfect for a long leisurely walk or finding a secluded spot to relax and soak up the sun. Anlin supplies beach towels, chairs and umbrellas for your relaxed, sun filled days. Ideally suited for family holidays or couples looking for a quiet beach getaway, Anlin Beach House offers a choice of bed & breakfast stay, or self-catering in one of our 4 accommodation options. Our rooms are decorated in a modern, contemporary style and include DSTV, free wireless internet connection, DVD player and are serviced daily. Not your typical Beach House, this eclectic fusion of African art and artefacts and contemporary furnishings creates a warm and inviting atmosphere, perfect for putting your feet up and enjoying long lazy days in a home from home. Anlin Beach House is approximately a 3 minute drive from the centre of town, and we also have a mini centre just 1 minute away that has a Supermarket, Pharmacy and Restaurant. Plett, as it is known by the locals, offers so much to see and do during your holiday that we recommend a minimum stay of 3-4 days in order to really relax & enjoy the surrounding area.
