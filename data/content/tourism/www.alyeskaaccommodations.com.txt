This town boasts one of the area’s finest ski resorts, not to mention the stunning natural beauty of mountains, water, and glaciers. Where is Girdwood? This town’s action revolves around the Alyeska Ski Resort, and our Girdwood rentals give guests easy access to the best of Alyeska. With Anchorage’s city center just under 40 miles away, Girdwood feels much more secluded than it is. It’s just a short drive to Whittier, a hub for many of the area’s best glacier tours and other boat activities in the Prince William Sound. Things to Do in Girdwood The Girdwood area offers something for visitors in every season. Winter guests can take advantage of Alyeska Ski Resort’s majestic slopes, and the Alyeska Aerial Tram is just as worthwhile for the scenery as it is for skiing. Keep an eye out for moose, bears, elk, foxes, bald eagles, and more at the Alaska Wildlife Conservation Center a few miles down the road. If you’re looking for a family-friendly hike, Virgin Falls is a quick and easy walk to a moss-covered waterfall. Girdwood also provides plenty of opportunities for fishing, biking, and other outdoor adventures. Visit one of our Girdwood cabin rentals for a taste of Alaska’s glory. Whether you’re here for a winter ski trip or a summer glacier tour, you won’t be disappointed in a Girdwood vacation!
