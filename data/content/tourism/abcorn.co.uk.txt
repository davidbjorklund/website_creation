Located in an impressive Victorian villa the Abcorn is spacious, pleasantly decorated and retains many of its original features. There are 7 accommodating and tastefully decorated guest rooms available. All rooms have en-suite shower and bathroom facilities, flat screen TVs and tea/coffee facilities. Free Wi-Fi is also provided. The Abcorn Guest House is situated just over a mile from the city centre. Holyrood Park, Dynamic Earth and the Royal Commonwealth Pool are all nearby. The free, private car park is large enough for all our guests cars. There is a bus stop just outside the Guest House ensuring quick and easy access to the main tourist attractions, via the excellent local bus service. The Abcorn is a completely non-smoking establishment.
