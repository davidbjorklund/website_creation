Around Llandudno Ty Mair Welcome to our guesthouse in Llandudno Richard and Mair would like to warmly welcome you to their family run  "Haven by the Sea". If you are looking for a friendly, relaxed and comfortable stay, look no further than the Hafan-y-Mor Guesthouse. Ideally situated in the centre of Llandudno, Hafan-y-Mor  is convenient for holiday makers, conference and theatre goers, business people and those just wanting a break from their busy lives. Often used as a base, Llandudno, the "Queen of  Welsh Resorts", is a perfect destination offering a number of attractions ranging from the traditional Punch and Judy, the Victorian pier, the beach and the Great Orme Park to the adreneline filled activities such as Snowdonia Zip World and Bounce Below. A comprehensive list with links can be found on our local attractions page. Each of our six guest bedrooms are tastfully decorated and equipped with everything you require for an enjoyable stay. In compliance with the law, all our rooms are strictly non smoking providing for a safe, clean and fresh environment to all our guests. Our hearty Welsh breakfasts are freshly cooked using local produce whenever possible and served in our bright and charming dining room. If you have any special dietary requirements such as vegetarian, gluten free or allergies, please let us know in advance so we can ensure your breakfast experience with us is an enjoyable one. To see what our past guests are saying about us, please look at the latest reviews on Tripadvisor. And don't forget, in order to keep our prices competitive we advise our guests to book direct either via our own website booking  link or by telephone  and avoid booking  agents like booking. com.
