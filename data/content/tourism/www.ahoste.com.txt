Long Stay - Short Stay / YOU NAME IT Have a nice stay in Ghent. • • • • / slider content column The flats "Bij Adolf Hoste" in Ghent are serviced with your comfort and well-being in mind for a seamless relocation or temporary housing. Serviced flats in the heart of Ghent Bernard Hebbelynck, manager of the building, welcomes you in one of his flats located in the Galgenberg. Cinemas, opera house or concert hall are within walking distance. Many shops such as a chemist, laundrette, grocer, baker and butcher are only fifty metres away. A variety of restaurants and pubs are close by. Trams halt just around the corner and will take you in five minutes to the railway station or the city centre. An appreciated concept The serviced flats, although personalised, are all fully equipped for one, two and up to four people. They offer much more space and substantial savings compared to a hotel room. Each flat comprises:   A spacious living room with laminated floor, comfortable Italian designer chairs, flat screen television connected to four satellite dishes, high speed internet. Landline VOIP. A fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher and microwave. One or two bedrooms with double beds. One or two bathroom with shower/bath. An entrance hall. A separate toilet. A terrace with a lovely view. The flats are specially appreciated for the quiet surroundings and make you feel at home. In some flats you will find an extra sofa-bed. Household linen can be provided for and is changed weekly on request. There is only a limited number of units, but we offer all the facilities of larger buildings: elevator, sauna, private basement, personal safe and letterbox, washing machine and dryer (at your disposal in the basement if not in the flat) and a parking space in our spacious garage. The organisation Our office is located at the rear of the flats. We are at your disposal during working hours. The owner Bernard lives next door. No room service as in hotels.
