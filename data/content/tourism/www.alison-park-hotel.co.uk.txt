Providing comfortable accommodation in an Edwardian Arts and Crafts period building. Surrounded by mature gardens and with free on site parking for 12 cars.  Located on the edge of Buxton’s town centre, (just far enough from the hustle and bustle) but close enough that it’s a 5 minute walk away from the Buxton Opera House, Pavilion Gardens, and Pavilion Arts Centre.  All of which are regular venues for Buxton’s popular Festivals and other special events. The Alison Park Hotel is well situated for visiting Buxton or exploring the Peak District National Park and the Derbyshire’s Hills and Dales – therefore offering opportunities for walking, cycling or climbing. We Provide accommodation on a Room Only, Bed and Breakfast or Dinner, Bed and Breakfast basis so you are sure of finding arrangements to suit your needs.  The hotel welcomes children, with family rooms available or the option of adding foldaway extra beds, cots or even bunk beds where space allows! The Hotel accepts small well behaved pets by arrangement. We frequently offer special prices and tempting special breaks .  Also providing catering and venue facilities for small events.  Whether its an intimate wedding reception, a family reunion or an informal business meeting let us take care of the hospitality therefore you can focus on being together. The Alison Park Hotel Facilities Include: Hotel Restaurant and Bar, Conservatory with Terrace tables Private car parking aprox’ 12 Vehicles, (free of charge for guests) Hotel gardens Secure Free Wi-Fi (Where Available) Lounge Area Bike Storage Available Each hotel room has Free-view TV, Hot Beverage making facilities, Telephone and Hair-dryer. All Rooms Non – Smoking Catering for small groups, reunions, parties & celebrations “Over a Hundred Years of Hospitality” The history of the Alison Park Hotel.
