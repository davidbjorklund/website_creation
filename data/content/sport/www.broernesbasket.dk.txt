Tournament basket teams Herre 1/Men’s 1st : 3rd division Herre 2/ Men’s 2nd : Serie 1, øst Herre 3/ Men’s 3rd : Serie 3, øst Old boys : Old Boys 30+ Dame 1 /Ladies 1st : Serie 1, øst Dame 2/ Ladies 2nd : 2. division, øst Casual basket teams Herre 4/Men’s 4th plays casual basket.
