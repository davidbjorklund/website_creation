Ever wanted to learn all there is to kayaking, or just try it? Then join our 5 week introduction course. In those five weeks we'll teach you the basics of kayaking. You can choose which day you want to join, Monday, Tuesday or Wednesday, so don't hesitate and sign up below. The course starts from the 13th of May. Sign Up Canoe polo Canoe polo is a unique mix between waterpolo, rugby and kayaking with the best of all worlds. Sprint In sprint everything revolves around going as fast as possible using the perfect combination of technique and strength. Touring Euros Kano also does canoe tours, for example day tours on nearby rivers, but also holidays on the Swedish lakes. Whitewater Whitewater kayaking is all about paddling fast moving rivers and learning to use the current to your advantage. Association D.K.V. Euros is also an active association. We've dinner every Monday and we regularly have non-canoe related activities. Freestyle Freestyle kayaking is all about boat control and using it to do astonishing tricks on both flatwater and whitewater.
