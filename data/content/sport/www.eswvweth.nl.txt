WETH is the student windsurf association of Eindhoven which offers the ideal opportunity to do this around and in Eindhoven. Our association is open for beginners, intermediates and of course pro's. We offer surfing lessons and own surf equipment which you can use as a member. We organise several surfing weekends, BBQs and winter activities during the year. Are you beginner? Don’t worry we will teach you how to surf during the weekends! Are you intermediate or pro?
