Players can work under professional conditions enabling them to focus solely on their performance. The tennis academy takes care about the complex services around the player ( regeneration , sparing partners, fitness, balls, accommodation , private education or any other additional services ). For competition players we offer annual tennis programs , summer tennis camps , intensive full year tennis camps or a program focusing on a tennis scholarship on universities in USA .  Kids tennis programs  in our tennis academy are designed for players up to 10 years. For the youngest age categories we provide children trainings 2-5 times per week, summer kids camps and tennis camps . Hobby players can rent a tennis court during the whole year and can use the services of our tennis coaches.
